KETOROLAC


Ketorolac is a nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID) that is used to relieve moderate to severe pain. It belongs to a class of medications known as propionic acid derivatives. Ketorolac works by reducing the production of certain chemicals in the body that cause pain and inflammation. Ketorolac is available in various forms, including tablets, injections, and nasal spray. The specific form and dosage will depend on the condition being treated and the instructions provided by a healthcare professional. .
