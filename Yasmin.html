Yasmin


Yasmin is a brand name for a combination oral contraceptive pill. It is a prescription medication that contains two active ingredients: drospirenone, a progestin hormone, and ethinyl estradiol, an estrogen hormone. Yasmin is used as a contraceptive to prevent pregnancy.Yasmin works by preventing ovulation, the release of an egg from the ovary. It also thickens the cervical mucus, making it more difficult for sperm to reach the egg, and changes the lining of the uterus, reducing the likelihood of implantation. .
