CEFACLOR


price:100 Cefaclor works by inhibiting the synthesis of the bacterial cell wall, which is essential for the survival and integrity of the bacteria. By interfering with this process, cefaclor effectively kills the bacteria or prevents their growth.Cefaclor is effective against a range of Gram-positive and Gram-negative bacteria. It is commonly used to treat respiratory tract infections such as bronchitis, pneumonia, and tonsillitis caused by susceptible organisms. It is also effective against certain skin and soft tissue infections, urinary tract infections, and middle ear infections (otitis media).
