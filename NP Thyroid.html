NP Thyroid


NP Thyroid is a prescription medication that is used to replace or supplement thyroid hormone in individuals with an underactive thyroid gland, a condition known as hypothyroidism. It is a natural desiccated thyroid (NDT) product, meaning it is derived from porcine (pig) thyroid glands.NP Thyroid contains a combination of thyroid hormones, including both thyroxine (T4) and triiodothyronine (T3). These hormones are essential for regulating the body's metabolism, growth, and development.
