Naproxen


Naproxen is a nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID) that is commonly used for the relief of pain, inflammation, and swelling associated with various conditions. It belongs to a class of medications known as nonselective NSAIDs.Naproxen works by reducing the production of certain chemicals in the body that contribute to pain and inflammation. It is effective in relieving pain caused by conditions such as arthritis, menstrual cramps, musculoskeletal injuries, and other inflammatory conditions.
