AMLOKIND-AT


Amlokind-AT is a brand name for a medication that contains two active ingredients: amlodipine besylate and atenolol. Amlodipine is a calcium channel blocker that works by relaxing blood vessels, while atenolol is a beta-blocker that works by slowing down the heart rate and reducing the workload on the heart. Amlokind-AT tablets are used to treat high blood pressure (hypertension) and angina (chest pain). The medication is available in different strengths, with varying amounts of amlodipine and atenolol in each tablet. The dosage and duration of treatment may vary depending on the severity of hypertension or angina and other individual factors.
