CILOSTAZOL


price:253 Cilostazol is a medication that belongs to a class of drugs known as phosphodiesterase inhibitors. It is primarily used for the treatment of intermittent claudication, a condition characterized by pain and cramping in the legs during physical activity due to inadequate blood flow. Cilostazol helps improve blood flow by widening the blood vessels and preventing platelets from sticking together, thus reducing the symptoms of intermittent claudication.
