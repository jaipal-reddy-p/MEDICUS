BEYAZ


Beyaz is a prescription medication that is a combination of three active ingredients: drospirenone, ethinyl estradiol, and levomefolate calcium. It is a type of oral contraceptive, commonly known as a birth control pill. The hormones drospirenone and ethinyl estradiol work together to prevent pregnancy by inhibiting ovulation (the release of an egg from the ovary), thickening cervical mucus to impede sperm movement, and altering the lining of the uterus to make it less receptive to implantation.
