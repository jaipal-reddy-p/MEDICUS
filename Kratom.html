KRATOM


Kratom is an herbal supplement derived from the leaves of the Mitragyna speciosa tree, native to Southeast Asia. It has been traditionally used for its stimulant and analgesic (pain-relieving) properties. Kratom contains active compounds called alkaloids, with mitragynine and 7-hydroxymitragynine being the primary ones. Kratom is available in various forms, including dried leaves, powdered leaves, capsules, and extracts. It is often consumed orally by chewing the leaves, brewing them into a tea, or swallowing the powdered form. The effects of kratom can vary depending on the strain and dosage. .
