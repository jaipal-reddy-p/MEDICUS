JALYN


Jalyn is a prescription medication that combines two active ingredients: dutasteride and tamsulosin. It is used to treat the symptoms of benign prostatic hyperplasia (BPH), a condition characterized by an enlarged prostate gland. Jalyn is specifically prescribed for the treatment of symptomatic BPH in men. Dutasteride is a 5-alpha-reductase inhibitor that helps shrink the prostate gland, while tamsulosin is an alpha-1 blocker that relaxes the muscles in the prostate and bladder neck, improving urine flow.
