BANZEL 400 MG


It is specifically indicated for use in patients who are at least 1 year old. Banzel belongs to a class of medications called antiepileptic drugs (AEDs) or anticonvulsants. It works by stabilizing electrical activity in the brain, which helps to prevent abnormal or excessive electrical signals that can lead to seizures. The dosage and administration of Banzel can vary depending on the individual's age, weight, and response to the medication. It is typically available in tablet form for oral administration, and the tablets come in different strengths, such as 200 mg and 400 mg.
