GLUCOTROL


15.95 rs Glucotrol is a brand name for the medication glipizide, which belongs to the class of drugs known as sulfonylureas. It is an oral medication primarily used to help manage blood sugar levels in individuals with type 2 diabetes. Glucotrol works by stimulating the release of insulin from the pancreas and increasing the sensitivity of cells to insulin. Insulin is a hormone that helps regulate blood sugar levels by facilitating the uptake of glucose from the bloodstream into cells for energy.
