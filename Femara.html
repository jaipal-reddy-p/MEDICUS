FEMARA


Femara is the brand name for the medication letrozole. It belongs to a class of drugs known as aromatase inhibitors. Letrozole is primarily used in the treatment of hormone receptor-positive breast cancer, which is the most common type of breast cancer. Here are some key points to know about Femara (letrozole): Usage: Femara is prescribed for the treatment of postmenopausal women with hormone receptor-positive breast cancer. It is used as an adjuvant treatment (after surgery) or as a first-line or subsequent treatment for advanced breast cancer. .
