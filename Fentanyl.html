FENTANYL


Fentanyl is a potent synthetic opioid medication that is primarily used for the management of severe pain. It belongs to the class of drugs known as opioid analgesics. Fentanyl is available in various forms, including injections, transdermal patches, lozenges, nasal sprays, and buccal tablets. Here are some key points to know about fentanyl: Pain management: Fentanyl is typically prescribed for the management of severe pain in situations where other analgesics may not be effective. It is commonly used for post-operative pain, cancer pain, and chronic pain conditions. .
