BARHEMSYS


Barhemsys is a prescription medication that contains the active ingredient amisulpride. It is primarily used to prevent and treat postoperative nausea and vomiting (PONV) in adult patients. PONV is a common complication that can occur after surgery and is characterized by feelings of nausea and vomiting. Barhemsys belongs to a class of medications known as dopamine antagonists. It works by blocking the dopamine receptors in the brain that are involved in triggering nausea and vomiting. By inhibiting these receptors, Barhemsys helps to prevent and alleviate PONV symptoms.
