ALLEGRA 180 MG


Allegra 180mg is a brand name for a medication called fexofenadine hydrochloride. Each tablet contains 180mg of fexofenadine hydrochloride as the active ingredient. Fexofenadine is an antihistamine medication used to treat various allergic conditions such as hay fever, hives, itching, and allergic rhinitis. It works by blocking the action of histamine, a chemical released by the body during an allergic reaction that causes symptoms such as itching, swelling, and redness.
