KETOPROFEN


Ketoprofen is a nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID) that is commonly used to relieve pain, reduce inflammation, and decrease fever. It belongs to the propionic acid class of NSAIDs. Ketoprofen works by inhibiting the production of certain chemicals in the body that contribute to pain, inflammation, and fever. Ketoprofen is available in various forms, including oral capsules, tablets, extended-release capsules, and topical formulations such as gels or creams. The specific formulation and dosage will depend on the condition being treated and the healthcare provider's recommendation. .
