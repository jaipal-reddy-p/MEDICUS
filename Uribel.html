Uribel


Uribel is a medication that is used to relieve the symptoms of urinary tract irritation and discomfort. It is a combination product containing four active ingredients: methenamine, sodium phosphate monobasic, phenyl salicylate, and hyoscyamine sulfate. Uribel is primarily prescribed for the relief of pain, burning, urgency, and frequency associated with urinary tract infections (UTIs) and other urinary tract disorders.
