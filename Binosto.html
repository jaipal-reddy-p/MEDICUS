BINOSTO


Binosto is a prescription medication that contains the active ingredient alendronate sodium. It is primarily used for the treatment and prevention of osteoporosis in postmenopausal women and for the treatment of osteoporosis in men. Alendronate belongs to a class of medications called bisphosphonates, which work by slowing down the breakdown of bone and increasing bone density. Binosto helps to reduce the risk of fractures and improve bone strength in individuals with osteoporosis.
