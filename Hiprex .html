HIPREX


220.10$,Hiprex is a brand name for the medication methenamine hippurate. It is an antibacterial agent used to prevent and treat urinary tract infections (UTIs). Methenamine hippurate works by converting to formaldehyde in urine, which creates an acidic environment that helps prevent the growth and proliferation of bacteria in the urinary tract. It is effective against a wide range of bacteria commonly associated with UTIs.
