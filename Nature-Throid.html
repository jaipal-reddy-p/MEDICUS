Nature-Throid


Nature-Throid is a prescription medication used for the treatment of hypothyroidism, a condition characterized by an underactive thyroid gland. It is a natural desiccated thyroid (NDT) product, which means it is derived from the thyroid glands of pigs. Nature-Throid contains a combination of thyroid hormones, including both thyroxine (T4) and triiodothyronine (T3). These hormones are necessary for regulating the body's metabolism, growth, and development.
