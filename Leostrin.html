Loestrin


Loestrin 24 Fe is a prescription birth control pill that combines hormones to prevent pregnancy. It contains a combination of two hormones: ethinyl estradiol, which is an estrogen, and norethindrone acetate, which is a progestin. Loestrin 24 Fe is a monophasic pill, meaning that each active pill in the pack contains the same dose of hormones. The "24" in the name indicates that there are 24 active pills in a pack, followed by 4 inactive reminder pills. The "Fe" stands for iron, as these reminder pills contain iron supplements. .
