HALDOL


36 rs, Haldol is a brand name for the medication haloperidol, which belongs to the class of drugs known as antipsychotics. It is primarily used to manage symptoms of certain mental and mood disorders, including schizophrenia and acute psychosis. Haloperidol works by blocking dopamine receptors in the brain. Dopamine is a neurotransmitter that plays a role in various brain functions, including the regulation of mood and behavior. By blocking dopamine receptors, haloperidol helps to reduce psychotic symptoms, such as hallucinations, delusions, and disorganized thinking
