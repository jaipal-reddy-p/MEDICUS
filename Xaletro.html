Xarelto


Xarelto is prescribed for several different conditions. It is commonly used to prevent blood clots in individuals who have undergone hip or knee replacement surgery. It is also used to reduce the risk of stroke and systemic embolism in individuals with non-valvular atrial fibrillation, a type of irregular heartbeat. Additionally, Xarelto is approved for the treatment and prevention of deep vein thrombosis (DVT) and pulmonary embolism (PE). .
