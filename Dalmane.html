DALMANE


Dalmane is a prescription medication that contains the active ingredient flurazepam. It is a benzodiazepine, which is a class of drugs commonly used to treat insomnia (difficulty sleeping) and anxiety disorders. Flurazepam, the active ingredient in Dalmane, works by enhancing the effects of a neurotransmitter called gamma-aminobutyric acid (GABA) in the brain. This helps to promote relaxation, reduce anxiety, and induce sleep. Dalmane is typically prescribed for short-term use in the treatment of insomnia. It is usually taken orally as a tablet before bedtime.
