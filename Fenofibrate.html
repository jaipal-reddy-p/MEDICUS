FENOFIBRATE


Fenofibrate is a medication that belongs to a class of drugs known as fibric acid derivatives or fibrates. It is commonly prescribed under various brand names, including Tricor and Lofibra. Fenofibrate is primarily used to treat high levels of triglycerides and low-density lipoprotein (LDL) cholesterol in the blood, while also increasing levels of high-density lipoprotein (HDL) cholesterol. Here are some key points to know about fenofibrate: Usage: Fenofibrate is prescribed to help manage dyslipidemia, a condition characterized by abnormal lipid levels in the blood. It is often used in combination with lifestyle changes, including a healthy diet, exercise, and weight management. .
