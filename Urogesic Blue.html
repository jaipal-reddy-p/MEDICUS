Urogesic Blue


Urogesic Blue is a medication that is used to relieve the symptoms of urinary tract irritation and discomfort. It is a combination product that contains three active ingredients: methylene blue, hyoscyamine sulfate, and methenamine. Urogesic Blue is primarily prescribed for the relief of pain, burning, urgency, and frequency associated with urinary tract infections (UTIs) and other urinary tract disorders.
