DAPSONE


Dapsone is a prescription medication that belongs to the class of drugs known as sulfones. It has multiple uses and can be prescribed for various conditions. Dapsone is primarily used for the treatment of leprosy, a chronic bacterial infection caused by Mycobacterium leprae. It works by inhibiting the growth and multiplication of the bacteria, thereby helping to control the infection. Dapsone is often used in combination with other medications for the treatment of leprosy. Additionally, dapsone is also used for the treatment of dermatitis herpetiformis, a skin condition associated with celiac disease. It can help relieve the itchy rash and blisters associated with this condition.
