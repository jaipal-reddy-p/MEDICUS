JANTOVEN


Jantoven is a brand name for the generic drug warfarin sodium. It is an oral medication primarily used as an anticoagulant or blood thinner. Jantoven tablets are typically taken by mouth and are available in different strengths, including 1 mg, 2 mg, 2.5 mg, 3 mg, 4 mg, 5 mg, 6 mg, 7.5 mg, and 10 mg. The tablets are usually round or oval in shape and come in different colors to indicate the strength of the medication. For example, the 1 mg tablet is typically pink, the 2 mg tablet is green, the 2.5 mg tablet is tan, and so on. The specific color and markings on the tablets may vary depending on the manufacturer.
