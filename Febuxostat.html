FEBUXOSTAT


Febuxostat is a medication primarily used for the treatment of gout, a type of arthritis caused by the buildup of uric acid crystals in the joints. It is marketed under the brand name Uloric. Here are some key points to know about febuxostat: Mechanism of action: Febuxostat works by inhibiting an enzyme called xanthine oxidase, which is involved in the production of uric acid in the body. By reducing the production of uric acid, febuxostat helps to lower uric acid levels in the blood and prevent the formation of uric acid crystals. .
