Zaleplon


Zaleplon is a medication that is used for the short-term treatment of insomnia. Zaleplon is typically prescribed for individuals who have difficulty falling asleep. It is recommended for short-term use, usually for a few weeks or less, to help with transient or situational insomnia. It is taken just before going to bed and should only be used when the individual has a full 7-8 hours available for sleep to minimize the risk of residual effects upon waking. .
