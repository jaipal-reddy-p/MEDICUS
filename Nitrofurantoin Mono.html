Nitrofurantoin Mono


Nitrofurantoin mono (monohydrate) is an antibiotic medication that is used to treat and prevent urinary tract infections (UTIs). It belongs to a class of drugs known as nitrofuran antibiotics.Nitrofurantoin mono works by inhibiting the growth of bacteria in the urinary tract, thereby treating the infection. It is specifically effective against certain strains of bacteria commonly found in UTIs.
